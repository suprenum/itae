A little about us

At present, it is difficult to imagine the modern world without information technologies, which every year are increasingly being introduced into all spheres of human activity. That is why the mission of the Faculty of Information Technology and Engineering is to train qualified and competitive specialists capable of making competent decisions in the most difficult situations, leaders and workers in demand in the labor market.

Research work will be carried out not only by teachers, but also by students of the faculty. Students will take part in international, domestic and intra-university scientific conferences and exhibitions, and will publish articles. The faculty cooperates with the Tomsk Polytechnic University.

We are always glad to see you at our faculty of purposeful and ambitious young people, aimed at obtaining high-quality prestigious higher education, open to discussions and full-fledged dialogue.
