About Us

The faculty was organized in 2019 and includes the most experienced specialists in the field. The faculty has 2 doctors, 7 PhDs and 5 candidates of sciences.

The educational process at the faculty will be carried out on a full-time basis, both on a budgetary and on a contract basis, for the preparation of undergraduates and bachelors in the direction of Information and Communication Technologies. Licensing was obtained in this area.

The faculty has modern teaching and laboratory and research classrooms, multimedia equipment, and will also constantly increase computer and educational equipment.

Research work will be carried out not only by teachers, but also by students of the faculty. Students will take part in international, domestic and intra-university scientific conferences and exhibitions, and will publish articles. The faculty cooperates with the Tomsk Polytechnic University.
